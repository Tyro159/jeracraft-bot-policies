# Survival Database Assistant Terms of Service

Last updated: June 18, 2026

These Terms of Service describe the rules for using Survival Database Assistant, a Discord recruitment bot used by the Jeracraft staff team to process volunteer staff applications.

This document is a practical operating policy for the bot and is not legal advice.

## 1. What the bot does

Survival Database Assistant helps the Jeracraft staff team:

- Import staff applications from a connected Google Sheet.
- Create Discord forum review threads for applications.
- Send application-related direct messages to applicants.
- Track review status, staff notes, follow-up requests, and final decisions.
- Log recruitment actions to a configured staff audit channel.

## 2. Who may use the bot

The bot is intended for use inside authorized Jeracraft Discord servers. Some commands may be available to reviewers or staff members, while final decision commands may be limited to management roles.

You may not use the bot to harass applicants, spam users, bypass Discord permissions, impersonate staff, or misuse application data.

## 3. Applicant communications

By submitting a staff application and providing your Discord user ID, you understand that the bot may send you direct messages related to your application, including:

- Confirmation that your application was received.
- Requests for follow-up information.
- Eligibility or rejection notices.
- Acceptance notices and onboarding information.

If you do not want to receive direct messages from the bot, you may block the bot or contact server management. Blocking the bot may prevent the team from sending application updates through Discord.

## 4. Application decisions

The bot helps organize recruitment review, but it does not guarantee acceptance into any staff team. Final recruitment decisions are made by authorized Jeracraft staff or management.

The team may reject or close applications for eligibility, conduct, incomplete information, department fit, server needs, or other recruitment reasons.

## 5. User responsibilities

When using or responding to the bot, you agree not to:

- Submit false, misleading, abusive, or unlawful content.
- Share private information that is not necessary for recruitment.
- Use the bot to send spam, threats, harassment, or malicious links.
- Attempt to access application data you are not authorized to view.
- Interfere with the bot, Discord, Google Sheets, or related systems.

## 6. Availability

The bot is provided as-is for internal recruitment support. It may be unavailable, delayed, restarted, modified, or discontinued at any time.

## 7. Discord and third-party services

The bot operates through Discord and Google services. Your use of Discord remains subject to Discord's own terms, policies, and community guidelines. Google services may also process application data through the connected Google Sheet.

## 8. Changes to these terms

These terms may be updated as the bot changes. Continued use of the bot after an update means you accept the updated terms.

## 9. Contact

For questions about the bot, application status, data requests, or these terms, contact Jeracraft server management through the official server support or management channels.
