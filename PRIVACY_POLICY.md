# Survival Database Assistant Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Survival Database Assistant, a Discord recruitment bot used by the Jeracraft staff team, collects, uses, stores, and deletes application-related data.

This document is a practical operating policy for the bot and is not legal advice.

## 1. Data the bot collects

The bot may collect and store the following information when you submit or discuss a staff application:

- Application responses imported from the connected Google Sheet.
- Discord username provided in the application.
- Discord user ID, if provided in the application or linked by a reviewer.
- Minecraft username.
- Selected department.
- Age or eligibility responses submitted in the application.
- Portfolio links, skills, interests, and written answers.
- Moderation scenario answers, if applying for moderation.
- Staff notes, follow-up requests, status changes, and final decisions.
- Direct messages you send to the bot in response to application follow-ups.
- Attachment URLs from direct messages sent to the bot.
- Basic audit log entries about recruitment actions, such as who linked an applicant, requested follow-up, accepted, rejected, or changed status.

## 2. How the bot uses data

The bot uses application data to:

- Determine whether an application meets basic eligibility requirements.
- Create internal review threads for staff applications.
- Notify relevant department roles about new applications.
- Let reviewers request follow-up information.
- Send applicants application updates by Discord direct message.
- Record staff notes and applicant replies for recruitment review.
- Track application status, such as intake, staff review, management review, accepted, or rejected.
- Maintain an audit trail for recruitment actions.

## 3. Where data is stored

Application data may be stored in:

- The connected Google Sheet used for application intake.
- The bot's local SQLite database.
- Discord forum threads created for application review.
- Discord direct messages between the bot and applicants.
- A configured Discord audit/log channel.

The bot does not intentionally sell applicant data or use it for advertising.

## 4. Who can access data

Application data may be visible to authorized Jeracraft staff, reviewers, and management with access to the relevant Discord channels, Google Sheet, or bot database.

Certain commands may allow reviewers to add notes, link Discord IDs, request follow-up information, or view application notes. Final decision commands may be limited to management roles.

## 5. Data sharing

The bot uses Discord and Google services to operate. Data may be processed by those services according to their own terms and privacy policies.

Application data is not intentionally shared with unrelated third parties except when required for bot operation, server administration, safety, legal compliance, or at the applicant's request.

## 6. Data retention

Application data may be retained for recruitment records, staff accountability, moderation history, and audit purposes.

Rejected or accepted applications may remain in the database, Discord review threads, audit logs, or Google Sheet unless deleted by server management.

If the bot is discontinued, management should delete or archive stored application data that is no longer needed.

## 7. Deletion and correction requests

Applicants may request that their application data be corrected or deleted by contacting Jeracraft server management through official server support or management channels.

Some records may be retained when needed for server safety, abuse prevention, audit history, dispute handling, or legal compliance.

## 8. Direct messages

If you provide your Discord user ID, the bot may send direct messages about your application. If you reply to the bot, your reply may be copied into the internal application review thread and stored with your application notes.

If your Discord privacy settings block bot direct messages, the bot may be unable to send application updates.

## 9. Security

The bot is intended to store data only in controlled systems available to authorized staff. Server management should protect the bot token, Google service account key, SQLite database, and Discord channel permissions.

No system can be guaranteed completely secure. If a data issue is discovered, server management should take reasonable steps to investigate and respond.

## 10. Children's privacy

The bot is intended for Discord users who are eligible to use Discord and apply for Jeracraft staff roles. The application workflow currently includes age eligibility checks.

Applicants should not submit unnecessary sensitive personal information.

## 11. Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated as the bot or recruitment process changes. The latest version should be made available wherever the bot's privacy policy link is listed.

## 12. Contact

For privacy questions, deletion requests, correction requests, or concerns about application data, contact Jeracraft server management through the official server support or management channels.
